💡 What is DeepDrift/ODD?

DeepDrift/ODD is like a detonation sensor for AI. It doesn't listen to what the model says, but how it thinks. If there's internal "tremor" (high Semantic Velocity) - expect nonsense on output. DeepDrift operates in the pre-output regime, detecting instability before any token is emitted.

⚠️ Demo Transparency: This demo visualizes synthetic hidden trajectories for educational purposes. The ODD core, calibration procedure, and decision logic are identical to those used with observed model activations. For production use, export real hidden states from your PyTorch models (see documentation below).
